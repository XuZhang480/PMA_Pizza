Margherita Pizza

Simple, fresh, and traditional.

Overview

Margherita pizza is known for its balance of tomato sauce, fresh mozzarella, basil leaves, and a thin crust. It is often chosen by people who enjoy a lighter and more classic flavor profile.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh mozzarella
  • Fresh basil
  • Olive oil
  • Thin crust

Flavor Profile

Mild, fresh, and slightly creamy, with herbal notes from basil and a clean finish.

Best For

People who like classic Italian-style pizza and simple ingredients.
