Pepperoni Pizza

Savory, rich, and crowd-pleasing.

Overview

Pepperoni pizza is one of the most popular pizza styles in the United States. It combines tomato sauce, mozzarella cheese, and slices of pepperoni that become slightly crisp during baking.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Medium crust

Flavor Profile

Rich, meaty, slightly spicy, and salty, with strong umami notes.

Best For

People who enjoy bold flavor and a classic American pizza choice.

This is the best choice for vegetarians.
