Veggie Pizza

Colorful, hearty, and vegetable-forward.

Overview

Veggie pizza highlights a variety of vegetables on top of a cheese and tomato base. Common toppings include bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, and spinach.

Ingredients

  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Mushrooms
  • Olives

Flavor Profile

Fresh, earthy, and slightly sweet, with varied texture from the vegetables.

Best For

People who want a lighter pizza with more vegetables and a more diverse texture.
